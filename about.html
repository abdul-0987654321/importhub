Our Story

How ImportHub Was Born

ImportHub started with a simple frustration: finding genuinely premium tech accessories in Pakistan was hard. Cheap copies, unreliable sellers, and no single trusted source — it was a mess.

In 2017, we decided to fix that. We built direct relationships with manufacturers and distributors worldwide to bring authentic, high-quality products to Pakistan at fair prices — all with the simplicity of ordering on WhatsApp and paying cash on delivery.

Today, with 300+ carefully curated products and thousands of happy customers, ImportHub is the go-to name for anyone who takes their gear seriously.
